Projects

IOT based fire alerting System

The IoT-Based Fire Detection and Suppression System is designed to detect fire hazards in real-time and automatically suppress them using water. It integrates temperature and smoke sensors to monitor the environment, with a microcontroller processing the data to activate a water motor for fire suppression. The system also sends alerts via SMS or email using IoT protocols like Wi-Fi, ensuring timely notifications. This cost-effective and scalable solution enhances safety by combining early fire detection with automated water-based suppression, suitable for both residential and commercial settings.
